Prestige SF is a self-organized network of people with a common agenda, cause, and interest in sharing ideas, information and resources. The goal is to create one of a kind member driven experiance for the creative and business professionals in the San Francisco Bay Area. Our club was designed to be a hub for the forward thinking individual that cant be put in a box. We strive to push the culture forward by never compromising on quality, enhancing the comminity and making our own rules.

OUR STORY


Founded by two San Francisco natives, Prestige Social Club evolved from the idea that quality isnt something that can be negotiated. Their passion for the class, character and integrity of the city lead them to the realization that their contemporaries needed their own space. They set out to create a space that allowed the influencers of taste to engage with other members of the professional elite. With a long-standing history in the City, they have built relationships with some of the most influential figures in sports, entertainment and local politics. Nestled in between historic China Town and the Russian Hill neighborhood, the space is located int he epicenter of San Francisco culture. Motivated to bring a new wave of class and style to the Bay Area, Prestige is dedicated to member-driven growth, through a member-driven experiance. With our team of professionals and years of marketing and event production experiance, we have the ability to host your next private party or business related conference. Welcome to Prestige San Francisco, social club and event center!
